About Us

Cryptofully is a crypto trading and payment app that makes money (government-issued currency) and cryptocurrency work seamlessly together. With Cryptofully, we want to make crypto simple, so that people can use it for everyday payments - remittances, bills, airtime and even business purchases.

The core features includes:

  • Cryptofully Wallet: Store and spend currencies
  • Business Account/Pay - The safe and easy way for business to accept payments in cryptocurrency
  • Payment link - For businesses and p2p users to request for payment
  • P2P transfers - Allows users to transfer funds (trade) from CF account to another account Virtual card/Physical card
  • Earn Interest and borrow money using your crypto as collateral
  • Support more currencies and launch in other African markets

Our beta will allow users to send and request Bitcoin, and cash out directly to any Nigerian bank account (from anywhere in the world).

Besides simplifying crypto for everyday transactions, cryptofully is looking to demystify the concepts of cryptocurrency and blockchain by making it very simple and easy for everyone to embrace. They intend to do this by running several educational initiatives across their online channels.
